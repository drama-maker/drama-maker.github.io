Cookies with pieces of chocolate

  • 140g dark chocolate (crumbled)
  • 130g soft butter
  • 200g white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 180g cake flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees. Line one or two baking sheets (for 16 or 32 cookies from this batch) with parchment paper and set aside. In a clean and dry mixer, place the chocolate and crush it for 3 seconds at speed 6. Transfer it to another container and set aside. In the mixing bowl, combine butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Mix for 2 minutes at speed 3. Add baking soda, baking powder, and salt to the bowl, and mix for 40 seconds at speed 4. Add the chocolate and mix for 30 seconds at speed 2. Form dough into 16 or 32 balls (cookies will be larger with 16) and place them in the oven for 14 minutes.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Fold in chocolate chips. Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.

Caprese Salad

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Slice tomatoes and fresh mozzarella. Arrange on a serving platter with basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Vegetable Stir Fry

  • Assorted vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds. Add assorted vegetables and stir-fry until crisp-tender. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Serve over rice.

Mango Salsa

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Combine diced mangoes, red bell pepper, red onion, and jalapeño in a bowl. Add lime juice and chopped cilantro. Season with salt to taste. Mix well and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. In a skillet, cook sliced chicken until no longer pink. In the same skillet, melt butter and sauté garlic. Pour in heavy cream, add Parmesan cheese, and stir until smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Combine cooked pasta and chicken with the Alfredo sauce. Serve hot.
