Pasta Carbonara

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper

Cook spaghetti al dente. In a separate pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Mix eggs, cheese, and pepper in a bowl. Drain pasta and mix with egg mixture. Add pancetta. Serve immediately.

Greek Salad

  • 1 cucumber
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 200g feta cheese
  • Black olives

Chop vegetables and feta. Combine in a bowl. Add olives. Drizzle with olive oil. Toss gently. Serve chilled.
